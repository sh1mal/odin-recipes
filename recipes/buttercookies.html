Description

Butter cookies (or sablés) are a classic pastry staple defined by their high butter content, which creates a rich, melt-in-your-mouth texture. Unlike sugar cookies, which are often sturdy and chewy, butter cookies are delicate, crumbly, and rely on quality vanilla and butter for their signature flavor.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • sugar
  • egg

Steps

  1. cream the butter
  2. cream the sugar
  3. bake
